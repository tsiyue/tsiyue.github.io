# 👋 About Me
**Hello! I'm Siyue Teng (滕思玥).** 👋 I’m a **third-year Ph.D. Student** at the **[Visual Information Laboratory](https://vilab.blogs.bristol.ac.uk/)**, **[University of Bristol](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/)**. I am supervised by **[Dr. Aaron Zhang](https://fan-aaron-zhang.github.io/)** and **[Prof. David Bull](https://david-bull.github.io/)**. My research interests include **Video Coding, Gaussian Splatting, Generative Modeling**.

🎓 Prior to that, I earned an **master's degree** from the **School of Engineering Mathematics and Computer Science, University of Bristol**, graduating with Distinction. I received my **bachelor’s degree** from the **School of Electronic Engineering, Xidian University**.
